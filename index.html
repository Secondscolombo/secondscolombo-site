The garden.

Passion fruit vines twined overhead. Mango trees that drop fruit in season. Two squirrels who run the place. Butterflies most mornings. The small birds the locals call pani kurulu — sunbirds, technically — hovering at the flowers.

They look like hummingbirds but aren't. They live here. So do you, for now.

The pool.

Open 6 a.m. to 11 p.m. Free for guests. Free towels. Free for non-guests if they're eating with us.

Warmer at sunset than at sunrise.

The reception fish.

A Siamese fighting fish lives in a glass bowl on the front desk. Solitary by design. Iridescent when the light hits him.

Eleven Years · Itemized

2014 Opened on this lane in Kirulapone. Same building. Mostly the same team. Eleven years and counting.
2019-21 "Pay-as-you-can" through the pandemic. Guests paid what they had. Embassies sent us stranded nationals. We came out of it certain we wanted to keep doing this.
2021 Renamed to Seconds. Because life is a matter of them. The name stuck.
2022 Switched to solar. The only hostel-grade hotel in Sri Lanka running on renewable energy. We have the certificate. We don't put it on the wall.
2022 Started recycling guest plastic into clothing. Bottles in. T-shirts out. A few thousand of them so far.
2022 The in-house restaurant was officially established. For house guests, then. For everyone, soon.
2025 30,000+ guests through the gate. Counted. Most stayed at least one night longer than they planned.
2026 Vinya Garden opens to the public. The garden is no longer a secret.

things people ask

Before you book.

Where exactly are you?
44 Kirulapone Avenue, Colombo 5. The 138 bus stops at the gate. Five minutes' walk to Kirulapone Railway Station. A short tuk-tuk to Galle Face, Viharamahadevi Park, or Wellawatte Beach.
How far from the airport?
About 35 km. 45 to 60 minutes by car depending on traffic. We arrange private pickup with a driver who tracks your flight, around USD 21–25. Or take a PickMe / Uber for LKR 4,000–5,000.
Is breakfast included?
Only if you book a "breakfast included" rate. When it is — it's a savoury Sri Lankan plate: cardamom dhal curry, our daily-baked bread, a half-cooked egg, and top-grade Ceylon tea or coffee. The bread comes out of the oven each morning. You can smell it from the garden.

If you booked without breakfast, no problem — the full Vinya Garden à la carte menu is available from 8:15 a.m.
Is your flaming kottu spicy?
No. Non-spicy by design — suitable for pregnant women, children, patients, and the elderly. The flavour comes from a six-hour homemade chicken bone broth, Sri Lankan farm cheese, lightly smoked egg, and quality celery oil. No stock powder. No MSG. No shortcuts. Made to order, every order, every time.
Is the pool open to non-guests?
Through the Pool Dining Package at Vinya Garden, yes. Order food, swim, eat, swim again. Otherwise it's for hotel guests.
Is Colombo 5 safe?
Yes. Kirulapone is residential — markets, supermarkets, hospitals, a railway station, all within walking distance. We've hosted solo travellers (many solo women) for over a decade.
How do I pay?
LKR, credit card, Wise, or PayPal. Whichever you've got.
