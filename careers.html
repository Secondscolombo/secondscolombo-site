You’re not joining a job.
You’re joining a career.

Seconds Colombo is growing — and as the brand grows, so does the team structure. We promote from within. Every senior person at Seconds started on the floor. The path is real, but the timing is honest: roles open up when they open up. We are transparent about that.

Front office staff · housekeeper

Where every career at Seconds begins. You learn the operation from the ground up — guest experience, systems, and standards.

Senior staff · team lead

You own your shift, mentor newer staff, and take on expanded responsibility across multiple departments.

Supervisor

You run the floor. Shift oversight, quality control, guest escalations, and team coordination fall under you.

Assistant manager

Operations, staffing, supplier relationships, and performance reporting. You are the bridge between the team and senior leadership.

General manager

Full responsibility for the hotel and restaurant — financial performance, guest satisfaction, team leadership, and brand standards. A role built through years of demonstrated ownership at Seconds.

i.
always hiring

Front office staff & receptionist

full time

the role

You are the first face a guest sees when they walk through the blue gate — and the last person who makes sure the day ran cleanly. This role combines front-of-house hospitality with genuine operational and administrative responsibility. It is not a desk job — it is a full operations role for someone who wants to understand how a boutique hotel actually runs.

what you will do

  • Welcome and check in guests from across the world — warmly and professionally
  • Manage bookings, payments, and room assignments using our in-house system
  • Handle guest questions, requests, and local recommendations with confidence
  • Coordinate airport shuttles, laundry, and F&B requests for in-house guests
  • Open and close the shift — cash book, inventory check, handover notes
  • Respond to messages via WhatsApp and assist with late arrivals
  • Support day-to-day operational admin — supplier coordination, records, scheduling support
  • Assist with OTA inbox management (Booking.com, Airbnb, Hostelworld, Agoda)
  • Maintain accurate shift logs, payment records, and guest data

what we are looking for

  • Warm, professional manner — you make people feel at home immediately
  • Confident English — spoken and written (additional languages are a plus)
  • Comfortable with technology — phones, tablets, basic software
  • Organised and detail-oriented — good admin is as important as good service
  • Calm under pressure and able to manage multiple tasks simultaneously
  • Honest, punctual, and takes the shift seriously
  • Prior hospitality experience is welcome but not required — attitude over CV
← yes, really. write us a letter.

what you will learn

  • Professional front-office and hospitality operations at an internationally facing property
  • Guest communication, conflict resolution, and service recovery
  • Revenue handling, shift reporting, and property management workflows
  • Booking platform management across all major OTAs
  • Hotel operational admin — supplier management, record-keeping, scheduling
  • How a modern boutique hotel operates end-to-end across every department
ii.
always hiring

Housekeeper

full time

the role

Housekeeping at Seconds Colombo is not just cleaning rooms. It is the final step in the guest experience — the moment before someone walks in and forms their first impression of their space. We take this seriously, and we look for people who do too.

← sinhala speakers, especially welcome.

what you will do

  • Prepare rooms and dorm beds to a consistent, high standard before every check-in
  • Follow a structured daily cleaning checklist across all room types
  • Report maintenance issues, missing items, or guest damage promptly
  • Manage linen, towels, and amenity stock with care and minimal waste
  • Coordinate with reception for check-out times and early arrivals
  • Support the garden and common area upkeep during quieter periods

what we are looking for

  • Reliable, self-motivated, and thorough — you notice what others miss
  • Takes quiet pride in a room that looks exactly right
  • Trustworthy and respectful of guest privacy and belongings
  • Able to work efficiently within the turnaround window between guests
  • Prior experience in hospitality housekeeping is an advantage

what you will learn

  • Professional housekeeping standards for a Tourism Board approved property
  • Linen and inventory management across a busy boutique hotel operation
  • Guest experience design — how the physical space shapes every review
  • How to work as part of an integrated hotel operations team
